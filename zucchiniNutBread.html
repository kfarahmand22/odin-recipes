Zucchini Nut Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cup Almond Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Coconut Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Chopped Walnuts
  • 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Ground Cardamom
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Shredded Zucchini
  • 2 Tbsp Maple Syrup

Steps

  1. Mix together all wet ingredients in mixing bowl.
  2. Mix together all dry ingredients in sepreate mixing bowl.
  3. Slowly fold dry ingredients in to mixing bowl of wet ingredients.
